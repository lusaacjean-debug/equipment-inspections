No checklists here yet

This category is set up and ready \u2014 it just doesn't have any checklists in it yet. Lubrication work is route-based and consumable-based rather than component-based: oil grades, sample points, grease intervals, and top-up volumes, tracked per route or per asset rather than per inspection.

Add your first lube team checklist

Each checklist is just two files: a data file listing the sections/items, and a thin HTML page that loads it. Copy the pattern from the static equipment checklists:

assets/checklists/lube-route-A-data.js lube-team/lube-route-A.html

In the data file, set a unique storageKey and list your sections/items \u2014 the shared engine (assets/app.js) handles checking off items, notes, flags, progress, the summary table, sign-off, printing, and saving automatically. Then add a card for it on the home page and a link here.

Typical checklists to add

SUGGESTED

Oil Sampling Round

Sample point ID, oil grade/grade change verification, sample bottle labelling, lab submission log, contamination/water check.

SUGGESTED

Grease Lubrication Route

Grease points by asset, grease type, quantity dispensed, interval compliance, purge/relief valve check, over-greasing check.

SUGGESTED

Oil Top-Up & Level Check

Sight glass/dipstick level, top-up volume logged, correct oil grade confirmed, leak check at seals and breathers.

SUGGESTED

Filter & Breather Change

Filter change interval tracking, differential pressure check, breather desiccant condition, old filter inspection for debris.

SUGGESTED

Lube Storage & Handling

Storage area cleanliness, container labelling and segregation by grade, dispensing equipment condition, spill containment.

SUGGESTED

Oil Analysis Trend Review

Lab report review against alarm limits, wear metal trending, viscosity/TAN checks, follow-up action tracking.
