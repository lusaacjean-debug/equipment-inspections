Rotating Equipment

No checklists here yet

This category is set up and ready \u2014 it just doesn't have any checklists in it yet. Rotating equipment (pumps, compressors, motors, fans, gearboxes, conveyor drives) needs different check points than static equipment: vibration, alignment, bearing temperature, lubrication, and coupling condition rather than corrosion and thickness.

Add your first rotating equipment checklist

Each checklist is just two files: a data file listing the sections/items, and a thin HTML page that loads it. Copy the pattern from the static equipment checklists:

assets/checklists/pump-centrifugal-data.js rotating-equipment/pump-centrifugal.html

In the data file, set a unique storageKey and list your sections/items \u2014 the shared engine (assets/app.js) handles checking off items, notes, flags, progress, the summary table, sign-off, printing, and saving automatically. Then add a card for it on the home page and a link here.

Typical checklists to add

SUGGESTED

Centrifugal Pump Inspection

Alignment, seal/packing condition, bearing temperature & vibration, coupling guard, lubrication, suction/discharge pressure trends.

SUGGESTED

Electric Motor Inspection

Insulation resistance, winding temperature, bearing condition, vibration signature, terminal box integrity, cooling fan/airflow.

SUGGESTED

Gearbox & Coupling Inspection

Oil condition/analysis, gear mesh noise, seal leakage, coupling alignment, mounting bolt torque, guard condition.

SUGGESTED

Compressor Inspection

Valve condition, cylinder/rotor clearances, cooling system, safety relief valves, vibration & thermal trending.

SUGGESTED

Fan & Blower Inspection

Impeller/blade condition, bearing housing, belt/drive tension, guarding, vibration, ductwork connections.

SUGGESTED

Conveyor Drive Inspection

Drive pulley/lagging condition, brake system, gearbox, motor, belt tracking and tension, guarding.
